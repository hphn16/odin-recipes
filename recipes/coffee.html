Vietnamese Iced Coffee

A traditional cup of iced coffee made from Cafe du monde chickory coffee with sweetened condensed milk

A very tasty cup of coffee. Once you taste it, you will wonder where has this been all your life! Looking for something strong? Something weak? Something sweet? Something bitter? This drink has it all

Ingredients

  • Cafe du Monde coffee
  • Sweet condensed milk
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew coffee with your preferred method. But tradionally, a phin is used for this process. It's sort of like pulling a shot of espresso
  2. Add sweet condensed milk to your desired level of sweetness
  3. Mix well
  4. Add ice cubes
  5. This can also be had as a cup of hot coffee
  6. Enjoy!
