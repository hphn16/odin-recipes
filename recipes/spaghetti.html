Japanese Spaghetti

Ketchup flavored spaghetti with meat or meat substitude of your choice

A very simple dish incorporating items you may already have in your pantry. Japanese spaghetti or Nepolitan spaghetti is made with ketchup rather than pasta sauce. It only requires a few ingredients and can be prepared relatively quick if you want a quick and filling meal.

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Pasta (any pasta of your choice but usually spaghetti works best)
  • Protein of your choice, in this case I'll be using sausage
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Mushroom
  • Green/red bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Butter
  • Egg (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a pot, boil water, add a pinch of salt so the pasta doesn't stick
  2. In a pan, heat up some cooking oil and sautee onions until clear
  3. Add sausage, bell peppers, mushrooms, and garlic
  4. Cook for about 3 minutes, or until sausages are cooked
  5. Add pasta
  6. Add ketchup and soy sauce
  7. Mix well
  8. Add butter to make it more creamy and delicious
  9. Optional, but you can fry and egg and top it off. Tabasco sauce goes really well with this dish!
